Let me introduce myself...

I’m Hanabel, a passionate individual with a love for bringing art to life in the digital realm. Currently, I’mworking towards a B.S. in Computer Science at the University of Massachusetts - Amherst. In my free time I enjoy drawing, graphic design, photography, and playing the piano. I'm also passionate about positive social change, community development, and learned leadership.

What have I been up to?

I'm currently co-facilitating the college course EDUC491R - "Residential Education and Community Development". It's a course about the residential curriculum, positive social change, and leadership, all of which I'm passionate about!
As an orientation coordinator, I've had the awesome opportunity to help over 600 fresh faces settle into college life smoothly. From leading networking groups, tours, and presentations, I've made sure everyone feels welcomed and connected right from the start.
Check out my research project! Delving into online discussions on Reddit within Asian and Black communities, I explored perceptions of STEM fields. It's all about understanding diverse perspectives and shaping future research in this dynamic field.
I'm all about bringing our residential community together through fun floor and building events that really amp up the vibes. I've also been a support system for my residents, helping them navigate college life and providing resources to help them succeed. Overall, I'm really passionate about leadership and the power of positive social change.
At HackUMass 2023, I delved into the world of iOS app development. With 'What’s the Word?' I conceptualized and developed an app geared towards connecting individuals with shared hobbies, fostering new friendships and community bonds. Meanwhile,'TaskTail Towers' emerged as a fun side project, blending gamified productivity with whimsical hand-drawn icons and characters. Both experiences pushed my skills in Swift and SwiftUI, and I'm proud to have contributed to projects that aimed to enhance users' lives in unique and engaging ways.
